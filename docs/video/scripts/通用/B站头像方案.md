# B站头像方案（龟+爬宠 双线升级版）

---

## 设计背景

频道内容从"纯龟类"扩展到"龟 + 爬宠"（守宫/蝎子/蛙/蝾螈等）。原极客乌龟头像只代表龟，需要升级为能代表"整个爬宠生态"的形象。

核心人设：互联网极客 × 爬宠爱好者（用 debug 思维养爬宠，数据养宠，玄学养龟）

---

## 推荐方向：方向 B 守宫主角（极客守宫）

```
1:1 square avatar for Bilibili profile, circular crop friendly

A cute cartoon leopard gecko (or crested gecko) wearing round
nerdy glasses, sitting calmly facing forward. The gecko's body
is orange with dark spots, big friendly eyes behind the glasses.
A small glowing green terminal/code symbol (like ">_" or a small
screen) floating subtly near the gecko.

Simple clean background, soft gradient.

Visual style: kawaii anime, bold clean outlines, minimal detail
Color scheme: warm orange gecko, black glasses, subtle green code glow
Mood: nerdy cute, approachable, tech-savvy reptile keeper
```

**为什么推荐：** 守宫是整个"爬宠"品类的视觉代表，辨识度比龟更"异宠/爬宠"。极客眼镜保留人设。缩到很小也清晰。

---

## 备选方向

### 方向 A：多物种拼图（最全面）

```
1:1 square avatar for Bilibili profile, circular crop friendly

A circular emblem with four small reptile/exotic pet icons
arranged around the center like a clock:
- Top: a small turtle
- Right: a leopard gecko
- Bottom: a scorpion
- Left: a horned frog
In the center, a glowing green circuit/node symbol connecting them,
like a tech network hub.

Clean solid background, bold simple icons.

Visual style: emblem/badge style, flat design, minimal
Color scheme: each species in natural color, green circuit accent
Mood: comprehensive reptile keeper, tech/data theme
```

**适合：** 想全面体现"龟+爬宠"多品类。缺点：元素多，缩到头像尺寸可能糊。

---

### 方向 C：龟蜥并肩（双线平衡）

```
1:1 square avatar for Bilibili profile, circular crop friendly

A cute cartoon turtle and a leopard gecko sitting side by side
facing forward, both with simple round glasses, looking friendly.
The turtle is on the left, gecko on the right. Simple solid
background.

Visual style: kawaii anime, bold clean outlines
Color scheme: green turtle + orange gecko, neutral background
Mood: dual-content channel, turtle + reptile keeper
```

**适合：** 龟和爬宠内容五五开，想两者都不淡化。缺点：两个主角抢视觉焦点。

---

### 方向 D：抽象极简符号

```
1:1 square avatar for Bilibili profile, circular crop friendly

A minimalist geometric logo: a stylized hexagon (suggesting a
turtle shell / circuit node) with a small gecko silhouette
curved along its edge. One small green pixel heart in the center.
Clean solid dark background.

Visual style: modern minimalist, geometric, logo-like
Color scheme: white silhouette on dark background, green accent
Mood: clean, professional, tech-forward brand
```

**适合：** 追求设计感和缩小后的清晰度。缺点：辨识度需要时间积累。

---

## 选择建议

| 你的情况 | 推荐方向 |
|----------|----------|
| 爬宠内容占比上升，想凸显"爬宠"品类 | **方向 B（守宫主角）** |
| 龟和爬宠五五开，都要保留 | 方向 C（龟蜥并肩） |
| 想全面体现多物种 | 方向 A（多物种拼图） |
| 追求品牌设计感 | 方向 D（抽象符号） |
