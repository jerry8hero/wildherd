## 08 | 为什么不上来就养睫角守宫——地栖 vs 树栖

**对应文案：** "睫角守宫确实好看——大眼睛、树栖型、颜色也丰富。但豹纹守宫是地栖型，睫角守宫是树栖型。地栖型意味着它住在地面，你随时能看到它。树栖型意味着它大部分时间躲在树皮缝隙里，你可能翻半天缸才找到它在哪儿。"

```
16:9 horizontal illustration, split comparison

Left panel: a leopard gecko fully visible, walking on flat sand
substrate in a low horizontal terrarium, easy to see and observe.

Right panel: a crested gecko partially hidden behind vertical bark
in a tall terrarium, only its head and one eye peeking out, hard
to spot among the branches and leaves.

Visual style: clean comparison layout, educational
Color scheme: warm orange (left) vs cool green-brown (right)
Mood: practical difference, easy to understand
```

**对应文案：** "热区保持在 30 到 32 度，冷区保持在 24 到 26 度。温度梯度很重要，让它自己选择待在哪里。"

Visual style: clean scientific diagram, clear temperature zones

## 13 | 饲养要点——钙粉补钙

**对应文案：** "每次喂食前在虫子身上撒一层钙粉，每周补一次含 D3 的维生素粉。这是新手最容易忽略的步骤——不补钙会导致代谢性骨病，骨头变软、畸形，严重的话不可逆。"

```
16:9 horizontal illustration

Left: a hand shaking a small white bottle, dusting calcium powder
onto mealworms in a cup. White powder visibly coating the worms.
A vitamin D3 supplement bottle nearby.

Right: two gecko silhouettes side by side — one standing strong
with straight healthy legs, one with visibly curved bent legs
showing metabolic bone disease deformity.

Visual style: instructional with medical illustration element
Color scheme: warm tones, white powder, subtle red warning on right
Mood: important care step, health warning
```
